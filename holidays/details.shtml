Trip Highlights

  • Visit Shanti Stupa and Leh Bazar
  • Visit Sangam, Magnetic Hill, Gurduwara Pathar Sahaib
  • Visit Pangong Lake, situated at the height of 4350 meters (14270 ft) in the Changthang region of Leh district.

Itinerary in Brief

  • Day 01 : Arrive Leh
  • Day 02 : Leh Sightseeing
  • Day 03 : Leh - Pangong - Leh (300 Kms)
  • Day 04 : Depart Leh
View Itinerary

Itinerary in Detail

  • Day 1
    • Arrive Leh - Shanti Stupa, Leh Palace & Central Asian Museum

      Leh by flight, you will meet our representative at airport and transfer to Hotel, traditional welcome at the hotel with Khataks and welcome tea and coffee. Full day rest to get acclimatized to the high altitude of 3524 Meters (11,500 ft). Later in the evening visit Shanti Stupa and Leh Bazar for orientation.

    • Meals - Tea / Coffee, Dinner
    • Overnight at hotel.
  • Day 2
    • Leh Nature Tour - Sangam, Magnetic Hill, Gurduwara Pathar Sahaib

      After breakfast, drive towards east of ladakh on Srinagar- Leh Highway (NH1) to visit Sangam which is the confluence of 02 Rivers Indus River and Zanskar River than on the way back visit Magnetic Hill ( the hill that defies the gravity) and later to 12th Century Gurduwara Pather Sahib. Evening free to explore the market.

    • Meals - Breafast, Dinner
    • Overnight at hotel.
  • Day 3
    • Leh - Pangong - Leh (300 Kms)

      After breakfast, drive towards east of ladakh on Srinagar- Leh Highway (NH1) to visit Sangam which is the confluence of 02 Rivers Indus River and Zanskar River than on the way back visit Magnetic Hill ( the hill that defies the gravity) and later to 12th Century Gurduwara Pather Sahib. Evening free to explore the market.

    • Meals - Breafast, Dinner
    • Overnight at hotel.
  • Day 4
    • Tour Ends

      After early breakfast transfer to Kushok Bakula Leh airport for the return flight. Tour concludes.

    • Meals - Breafast
